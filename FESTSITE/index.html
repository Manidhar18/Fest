About QUANTUM

Quantum is the annual technical fest of college of engineering poonjar where megaminds from our engineering college come together and lock horns to find the ultimate geeks. It is loaded with events for students with all kinds of interests and skills like coding, wiring, sports,entertainment. Quantun'24 is loaded with venues and platforms for people with all sorts of skills to showcase and prove their mettle.Fasten your seatbelts and get ready to be mind-blown in the company of tech-sharks. If perspective is what you need, this is where you should be! QUANTUM'24 aims to be the first fully green and eco-friendly college fest in the state, with its motto and theme - GO GREEN!

About RIET

Rajamahendri Institute of Engineering and Technology was established in 2008 By Swarnandhra College of Engineering and Technology (SCET), an autonomous educational institution.

SCET is based on the principle, “Think Globally and Act Locally” and its associate institutions are located in diverse locations in Andhra Pradesh to achieve this goal. Rajamahendri Institute of Engineering and Technology is located in a scenic place on a calm hilly region with pure air and greenery congenial for any student to pursue learning with dedication. is a vibrant community indulged in scholarship and devoted to learning always remembering that education is a service to the nation and humanity.The College is affiliated to the prestigious Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) and APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (2015 Admission onwards).
